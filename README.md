## How to Use

### Before Your Event:
1. Open https://ignatius-42.github.io/social-qr-generator/
2. Select platform (LinkedIn/Twitter/Instagram)
3. Paste your profile URL
4. Generate QR code
5. Share to WhatsApp (create a solo group like "My LinkedIn QR")
6. Repeat for other platforms

### At Your Event:
1. Someone wants to connect
